Description

Refreshing drink made with natural or mineral water, sugar and lemon juice. Lemonade can also be made by letting lemon zest marinate in water for a few hours, then strain it and add a little lemon juice and sugar.

Recipe Information

  • 1 liter of water
  • 6 seedless lemons
  • Sugar to taste
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Squeeze the lemons and mix them with the water. Add sugar to taste and stir until dissolved. Serve in glasses with ice cubes.
