Odin Recipes

Description

It is a traditional dish from different Mediterranean cuisines that consists of baked pasta, so in essence it is the same as the recipe called timpana or pasta timpana

Recipe Information

For the sauce:

  • 600 g minced beef
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 or 3 cloves of garlic
  • 200 ml red wine
  • 800 g crushed tomato
  • 1 or 2 bay leaves
  • dried oregano to taste

For the bechamel:

  • 40g butter
  • 40 g wheat flour
  • 600 ml cold whole milk
  • Ground black pepper
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt

Steps

Recipe Information

  1. Preparation time: 25 minutes
  2. Cooking time: 1 hour and 30 minutes
  3. Total time: 2 hours and 15 minutes
  4. Servings: 6
  5. Category: main course
  6. Type of cuisine: Venezuelan
  7. Calories per serving (kcal): 776
